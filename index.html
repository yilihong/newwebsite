Dr. Yili (Kevin) Hong is an Associate Professor of Information Systems at the W. P. Carey School of Business at Arizona State University. He is also the director of the IS PhD Program and the co-director of the Digital Society Initiative. He is an Associate Editor of Information Systems Research and the Journal of the Association for Information Systems
